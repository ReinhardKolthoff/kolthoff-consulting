About Reinhard Kolthoff

Reinhard Kolthoff is a highly accomplished Business Transformation & Operational Excellence Leader with over 20 years of extensive experience. His career is marked by a deep commitment to driving efficiency, fostering innovation, and delivering measurable results across diverse industries.

  • Strategic & Growth Management: Crafting and executing strategies for sustainable business expansion.
  • Operational Excellence & Process Innovation: Implementing Lean, Six Sigma, and other methodologies to optimize processes.
  • Project & Program Leadership: Successfully leading complex projects from conception to completion.
  • Data-Driven Decision-Making & Analytics: Leveraging insights to inform strategic choices and improve performance.

Reinhard holds a Diplom Ingenieur in Naval Architecture and Maritime Engineering from the University of Applied Science - Bremen, Germany (2008). Based in Taytay, Rizal, Philippines, he brings a global perspective combined with local understanding to every engagement.
