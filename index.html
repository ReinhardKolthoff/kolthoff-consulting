About Us

Kolthoff Consultancy is driven by a singular vision: to infuse the precision and methodological rigor of German engineering into businesses striving for operational excellence. Founded by Reinhard Kolthoff, a highly accomplished Business Transformation & Operational Excellence Leader, our approach is rooted in over two decades of practical experience across diverse industries.

Reinhard holds a Diplom Ingenieur in Naval Architecture and Maritime Engineering from the University of Applied Science - Bremen, Germany (2008). Based in Taytay, Rizal, Philippines, he leverages global expertise with a deep understanding of local market dynamics to transform operational challenges into strategic advantages for our clients.
